## Real vs stub summary

- **Genuinely built & wired:** Overview (`/api/health/summary`), Insurance, Reports, Settings, the orphan Health-Intelligence page, the Add form for _insurance only_.
- **Hidden backend (built, never surfaced):** Health Readiness score, lab-marker reference-range flags, wellness recommendations, sleep summary, document-extraction picture. All reachable today via `/v1/health/intelligence` + `/v1/health/summary`/`recommendations`.
- **Stub tabs (permanently empty regardless of data):** Biometrics, Fitness, Nutrition, Labs, Medications, Documents, Analysis, Recommendations, Goals â€” all render `HealthTabEmpty`.
- **Broken paths:** `add` page POSTs to three non-existent endpoints (`medications`, `appointments`, `vitals`); legacy `lib/api/health.ts` calls `/api/health/overview|vitals|sleep|goals|...` that have **no route handlers** (only `summary` + `intelligence` proxies exist).
- **Genuinely missing (no engine):** Longevity, true Connected/Wearable ingestion into the health schema, Preventative-care backend.

## Highest-leverage repairs (no new infra)

1. Make the **Analysis** tab (and ideally a renamed **Readiness** tab) render `/v1/health/intelligence` â€” the readiness score, lab flags, supplements/meds, action items already exist (move/import the orphan `health-intelligence/page.tsx` component).
2. Wire **Recommendations** tab to `/v1/health/recommendations` (real data) instead of `HealthTabEmpty`.
3. Fix the **Add** page to only offer fields that have real write endpoints, or add the missing proxies; remove dead `lib/api/health.ts` calls.
4. Surface **Sleep** and **lab risk flags** as Overview cards from data already in `/v1/health/summary` + `/intelligence`.
