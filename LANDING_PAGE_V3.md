## 15-SECOND COMPREHENSION TEST (acceptance criteria)

A first-time visitor, after 15 seconds above the fold, can answer:

1. **What is it?** "An AI personal family office."
2. **Who is it for?** "Serious people who want wealth-grade financial + life intelligence."
3. **Why is it different?** "It knows my real data, reasons across my whole life, and is governed/auditable."
4. **What do I do next?** "Request access / try a sample profile."
   If any answer is unclear, the copy fails — re-tighten the headline + proof line first.
