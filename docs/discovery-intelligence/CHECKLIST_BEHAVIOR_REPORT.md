## Summary

The discovery flow IS a checklist (fixed FLOW + first-unanswered selection), and three rules let persona/ontology override the user's narrative: (a) the **bridge default to financial_independence** seeds a finance objective pre-conversation, (b) **primary = max confidence** ignores stated priority, and (c) the **"which matters most" answer is a no-op**. Together they make the seeded objective stick regardless of what the user emphasizes.
