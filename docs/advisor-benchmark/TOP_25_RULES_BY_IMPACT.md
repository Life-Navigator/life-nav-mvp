## Summary

- **The damaging rules are clustered in the number gate (#1–#7).** They protect the zero-fabrication
  guarantee but are _over-broad_: they reject whole turns and block legitimate document/tool/benchmark numbers.
  **MODIFY**, don't remove — preserve the guarantee, widen the inputs, and strip-not-reject.
- **Almost everything else is net-positive or neutral.** The advice rules (post-V4), structure, voice, and
  trust gates cost ~0 and several are LN's competitive edges (one-question, fabrication catching).
- **No rule should be REMOVED outright.** The highest-ROI change is MODIFYing rules #1–#4 (see
  `NUMBER_GATE_FORENSICS.md`).
