# Constraint-Aware Discovery (Phase 5)

**Date:** 2026-06-16 · Ask about the binding constraint, not the goal list.

## Behavior

When the narrative implies a constraint (time/money/health/energy/relationships), the question targets the constraint:

- **Health & life balance:** "What feels most out of balance right now — your health, your time, or your energy with the people you love?"
- **Financial stabilization:** "If we could relieve one thing in the next 90 days — the debt, keeping your housing secure, or the strain it's putting on your relationship — which would help you breathe again?"

These name the user's actual constraints (housing, debt, relationship strain, time, energy) rather than asking "what are your goals?".

## Sources

Constraints derive from the constraint FLOW step (`life.constraints`) + `emotional_signals` (money_stress → financial constraint; burnout → time/energy constraint; money_fine → finance not a constraint).

## Live evidence (v118)

Burnout and Crisis personas both received constraint-targeted questions naming their real constraints. ✅

## Honest scope

Constraint _severity_ is not quantified (no debt-to-income figure) — that needs real financial data, available post-discovery via the finance domain.
